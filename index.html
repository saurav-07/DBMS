IPL DataBase

We are creating a database of players playing in IPL for adding, removing, modifying the data of a player of a particular team.

Three tables will be made storing the data of the players, the country table and the IPL team table.

Only admin will access all the three tables and will have the eligibility to modify all three of them.

And, the team owners will be secondary admin and will have the access of profile of players of their team only and will have the modification eligibility.

And, players will have the access of their profile only and will be able to modify their profile.

Then, the website will have global users with just viewing eligibility of any team or player.

The data of the players and teams can be searched using the search bar.
